**Sahne 1: Sorun - Donanımdaki Ter ve Veri**

Slow motion, premium commercial style.*

**Sahne 2: Köprü - Verinin Yazılıma Aktarımı (Sırrı Vermeden)**

The smartphone screen lights up with a sleek, dark-themed notification that says "Recovery Offer: 20% Off Deep Tissue Massage". The person smiles and taps the screen. The background is a beautifully blurred luxury gym reception area.*

**Sahne 4: Global Vizyon**
> **AI Video Prompt:** *Cinematic aerial shot, abstract 3d animation. A dark, premium world map. ## VIDEO 2: AURA ERP "SMART SPA & GOD MODE" (Genel İşletme Videosu)
### Hedef Kitle: Tesis Sahipleri, Yatırımcılar
### Mesaj: Daha az efor, otonom satış, yüksek prestij ve müşteri bağlılığı

**Sahne 1: Sorun - Eski Nesil Ajanda Kargaşası**
> **AI Video Prompt:** *Cinematic medium shot. A stressed business owner sitting at a cluttered desk in a brightly lit, generic office. They are looking at an old-fashioned computer screen showing a boring grid calendar. They look tired and are holding their head in frustration. A phone sits on the desk, not ringing. Desaturated colors, slightly depressing mood.*

**Sahne 2: Çözüm - Aura God Mode ile Tanışma**
> **AI Video Prompt:** *Dynamic push-in camera movement. The scene transitions to a stunning, modern luxury office with floor-to-ceiling windows showing a city skyline at night. A confident, well-dressed business owner is looking at a sleek laptop. The laptop screen displays a futuristic, dark-mode dashboard with beautiful neon purple and blue graphs and a "God Mode" interface. The owner smiles with satisfaction. Rich, vibrant colors, premium corporate look.*

**Sahne 3: Otonom Zeka Çalışıyor (Predictive Recovery)**
> **AI Video Prompt:** *Abstract 3d motion graphics, photorealistic. A glowing brain made of digital neural networks is connected to a calendar interface. The AI automatically grabs a block of time and perfectly slots it into a glowing golden empty space on the schedule. Cash and coin icons subtly pop up confirming a sale. High-end financial technology style.*

**Sahne 4: Prestij ve Mutlu Müşteri (Retention)**
> **AI Video Prompt:** *Slow motion cinematic shot. A beautiful, high-end Spa reception desk made of dark marble. A receptionist is warmly handing a fresh towel to a smiling, relaxed customer. They are not looking at a computer; they are completely focused on human connection. In the foreground, out of focus, a tablet shows an automated "Sale Completed" notification in dark mode.*
